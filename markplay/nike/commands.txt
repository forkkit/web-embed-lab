
Must be run from ~/src/web-embed-lab:

To generate the formula in the "pf" subdirectory
./go/bin/auto-formulate ./markplay/nike/nike-auto-formulate.json ./markplay/nike/nikepf

To test it:
./go/bin/runner ./markplay/nike/nikepf/ ./examples/test-probes/
http://localhost:9090

to see the formulas:
curl http://localhost:9090/__wel_control

to change the formula: 
curl http://localhost:9090/__wel_control -X PUT --data "{\"current-formula\":\"Nike homepage formula\"}"

to run the exception test probe:

no errors:
./go/bin/runner ./markplay/nike/nikepf/ ./markplay/test-probes/ ./markplay/embed-scripts/no-op.js ./markplay/nike/nike-experiment.json 

with errors:
./go/bin/runner ./markplay/nike/nikepf/ ./markplay/test-probes/ ./markplay/embed-scripts/exception-thrower.js ./markplay/nike/nike-experiment.json
